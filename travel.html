Getting There

There are two main options:

If you’re flying from farther away, we recommend flying into Redding Regional Airport, which is about a 1 hour 15 minute drive to Shasta View Lodge.

Alternatively, if you’re flying into SFO or already local to the Bay, you can make it an easy 5-6 hour road trip, depending on traffic and how fast you drive.

If you do not feel comfortable renting a car or driving and do not have a carpool option with a friend, let us know on the RSVP form. Also, if you are happy to drive someone else, let us know on the RSVP form too.

Stop ideas along the way:

Old Sacramento Waterfront for a leg stretch and snacks

The Olive Pit for many olive samples and olive oil milkshakes

Sacramento National Wildlife Refuge for birds and nature

Sundial Bridge in Redding for a pretty river stop

Lake Shasta Caverns for more of an adventure
