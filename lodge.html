Staying On Site

You’ll be staying in one of the 9 cabins on the property at Shasta View Lodge.

If you are single, you may have one roommate, and since some of the cabins have multiple rooms, you may also have cabin-mates. We’ll do our best to place people with their friends or others they already know.

Cabins come with kitchenettes, bathrooms, and fireplaces.

For guests over 40

Hotel Recs

For guests staying off site and wanting a little more privacy or a quieter stay, here are the main nearby hotel options we’d suggest:

McCloud River Bed & Breakfast

Probably the nicest nearby option if you want something cozy and a little more low-key.

McCloud Mercantile Hotel

Historic boutique hotel vibes right in town, with a more polished feel.

McCloud Hotel

Another good nearby option if you want a classic inn feel and an easier early night.

McCloud Timber Inn

This one is basically right across the street, but it seems a bit more basic than the other options, and it is a little unclear whether they are currently taking reservations, so we’d probably recommend the options above first.
